## Notes on Style Selection

- **Volume everyday models** (CLA, A-Class, Golf, A3, 3 Series, Puma, Polo) likely benefit from the **lifestyle / real-world UK location** category — relatable context, matches how real customers imagine owning them
- **Prestige / halo models** (911, Urus, Roma, Range Rover, X5) work well in **moody studio** for clean hero imagery, or lifestyle for a more aspirational feel
- **Commercial / vans** (Transit Custom, Ranger, Transporter) could benefit from a third category: working context (loaded up, on-site, business environment). Not yet created.
