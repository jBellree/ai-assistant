# Magnitude Finance — Brand Colours

## Primary Palette (from official brand guidelines)

| Name | Hex | Usage |
|---|---|---|
| **Darkest Slate** | `#1A212E` | Primary background on all marketing materials |
| **Burnt Orange** | `#ED792B` | Logo mark, "FINANCE" wordmark, accents, highlights, title focus words |
| **White** | `#FFFFFF` | Main body text, headings on dark background |
| **Smooth Sand** | `#EBE7E3` | Light backgrounds, supporting use |
| **Apple Green** | `#0DB729` | CTA buttons ONLY |

## Notes

- Orange (`#ED792B`) is the hero accent. Keep orange highlight under 50% of any title's total length.
- Never put orange text on a white background — contrast is poor.
- The Darkest Slate background is non-negotiable on Magnitude branded content. It's the signature look.
- Apple Green is CTA only — do not use for decorative elements.
- All text boxes, cards, and featured sections must have **rounded corners** — no sharp edges.
