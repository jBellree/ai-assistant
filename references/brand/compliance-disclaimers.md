# Magnitude Finance — FCA Compliance Disclaimers

These are pre-approved text blocks for use on all marketing materials, social posts, and WhatsApp content.
Copy and paste the relevant block. Do not paraphrase or shorten these without checking with your compliance contact.

## Rules

- Any post mentioning a rate, monthly payment, or finance product MUST include the representative APR disclaimer
- Posts that are purely educational (e.g. "did you know you can finance a van?") can use the short disclaimer
- Never omit the FRN number (649675) from any public-facing content
- Never describe DSG or Magnitude as a "lender" — always "credit broker"
