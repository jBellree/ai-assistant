# DSG Group — Fonts (from official brand guidelines)

## Heading Fonts

| Level | Font | Weight | Colour |
|---|---|---|---|
| H1 | Montserrat | Semi-Bold | White or Deep Slate (match background) |
| H2 | Montserrat | Semi-Bold | White or Deep Slate (match background) |
| H3 | Montserrat | Medium | Brand accent colour (Teal, Azure Blue, Fuchsia, etc.) |

- H1 is the largest — all other text is sized relative to it
- H2 is approx 50% smaller than H1
- H3 is approx 30% smaller than H2
- Do not overuse H2 — it is a section heading, not a paragraph heading

## Body / Copy Font

**Fira Sans Light** (external materials)
- Bold text within body: **Fira Sans Medium**
- Internal use only (when Fira Sans unavailable): Calibri Light

## Sizing Guide (1080×1080 canvas)

| Element | Font | Weight |
|---|---|---|
| H1 | Montserrat | Semi-Bold |
| H2 | Montserrat | Semi-Bold |
| H3 | Montserrat | Medium (in brand accent colour) |
| Body | Fira Sans | Light |
| Bold body | Fira Sans | Medium |
| Quote mark | Montserrat | Semi-Bold (in brand accent colour) |

## Notes

- Never use serif fonts anywhere
- Quotes: large opening " mark in Montserrat Semi-Bold, in brand accent colour, top-left of quote block
- Rounded corners on all text boxes, image frames, and cards
