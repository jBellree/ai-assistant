# Business / Work

## DSG Financial Services (B2B)
- UK motor finance brokerage
- B2B model: DSG works with motor dealer franchises and independents
- B sources finance deals from a wide panel of lenders
- Products: HP, PCP, HP with Balloon (Lease Purchase), Interest-Only
- Covers regulated (Consumer Credit Act) and non-regulated (corporate, HNWI) finance
- Finance arranged from £10,000 to £5,000,000
- Vehicles: cars, vans, prestige, supercars, trucks, campervans, plant and machinery, LHD American cars
- Average funded deal earns the company approx £1,000

## Magnitude Finance (D2C)
- Direct-to-consumer arm of the brokerage
- Goal: grow D2C to 30-40% of total monthly payouts

## Monthly Targets
