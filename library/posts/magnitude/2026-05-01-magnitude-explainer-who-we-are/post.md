**Who are Magnitude?**
- Magnitude are part of the DSG Group

- Access to the same lenders as through DSG

**Problems Dealers Face**
- Customers shop online for finance before arriving
- You lose control of the finance process
- Some brokers redirect buyers to other vehicles

**What Magnitude Offers**
- Rates from 6.9% on Non-Regulated

- You stay in control. We still pay a fee

**How It Helps You**

- Avoids losing finance to external brokers

- Still earns you a return
