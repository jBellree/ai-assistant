# Content Guidelines

## Hard rules
- Never slag off the competition
- Never talk a dealership down. Dealerships are B's bread and butter
- Never go down the compliance route with content ideas. No compliance-focused posts
- Never be pushy or salesy. B's brand is clarity, not pressure
- Always explain finance products in plain English
- Always frame content around the customer's needs, not B's services

## Content sources
- Real daily work makes great content: financing older prestige vehicles with balloons, VATable vehicles with deferred VAT, vans, LHD American cars, provisional licence holders, companies, sole traders, partnerships, new startups, supercars with enhanced balloons, trucks, campervans, voters roll tips
- Use the Social Post Checklist (Social Post Checklist.docx) as a quality gate
- Use the Buyer Personas (Buyer Persona.docx) to target messaging

## Buyer personas
- Persona 1: "The Savvy Private Buyer" (32-55, family-focused, wants clarity and control)
- Persona 2: "The Confident Business Owner" (35-58, cash flow focused, wants efficiency)

## Content funnel
- TOFU: Awareness and curiosity
- MOFU: Education and belief-building
- BOFU: Trust and conversion
