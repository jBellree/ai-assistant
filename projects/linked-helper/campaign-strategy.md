# LinkedIn & Linked Helper Campaign Strategy for Brian Rothwell - DSG Financial Services

## Context

Brian Rothwell runs DSG Financial Services Ltd in Greater Manchester, specialising in **vehicle finance** for individuals and self-employed tradespeople/sole traders across **North West England**. His biggest USP is himself - personal, hands-on service with access to multiple lenders. He has LinkedIn Sales Navigator but is new to both LinkedIn marketing and Linked Helper. He can dedicate 30-60 mins/day. Goal: generate leads, build referral networks, and establish thought leadership.

## Campaign 1: "The Tradesperson's Trusted Broker" (Primary Lead Gen)

**Objective:** Connect with self-employed tradespeople, sole traders, and small business owners in the North West who are likely to need vehicle finance (vans, pickups, work vehicles).

**Sales Navigator Search Filters:**
- Geography: Greater Manchester, Lancashire, Merseyside, Cheshire, Cumbria
- Job titles: Plumber, Electrician, Builder, Joiner, Roofer, Landscaper, Plasterer, Painter, Decorator, Gas Engineer, Heating Engineer
- Company headcount: 1-10 (sole traders / micro businesses)
- Industry: Construction, Building Materials

**Linked Helper Workflow:** `Warm-up, Invite & Follow-up` template

| Step | Action | Delay | Details |
|------|--------|-------|---------|
| 1 | **Visit profile** | - | Auto-visit triggers a notification so they see your name |
| 2 | **Follow profile** | 1 day | Shows genuine interest |
| 3 | **Like recent post** | 1-2 days | Engages with their content if available |
| 4 | **Connection request** | 2-3 days | Personalised invite (see message below) |
| 5 | **Message 1** (if accepted) | 1 day | Warm intro, no pitch |
| 6 | **Message 2** (if no reply) | 4 days | Value-add message |
| 7 | **Message 3** (if no reply) | 7 days | Soft CTA |

**Connection Request Message:**
> Hi {firstName}, I spotted you're a {occupation} based in the North West - always good to connect with local tradespeople. I specialise in vehicle finance and help a lot of self-employed people get sorted with vans and work vehicles. No pitch, just happy to connect! - Brian

**Message 1 (Day 1 after acceptance):**
> Thanks for connecting {firstName}! Quick question - do you own or finance your work vehicle? I only ask because a lot of tradespeople I speak to don't realise how much they could save by structuring their vehicle finance properly. Happy to share a few tips if it's ever useful. Cheers, Brian

**Message 2 (Day 4 if no reply):**
> {firstName}, just a thought - I recently helped a {similar trade} in Manchester save over 2k on his van finance by switching from his bank's deal. If you're ever looking at upgrading or changing your vehicle, I can run the numbers for you in about 5 minutes. No obligation, no hard sell - just straight-talking finance advice.

**Message 3 (Day 7 if no reply):**
> Last one from me {firstName} - I won't keep bugging you! If you ever need vehicle finance advice (or know someone who does), I'm always here. I've got access to multiple lenders so I can usually find something the banks can't. Have a great week!

## Campaign 4: "The Content Engagement Booster" (Authority Building)

**Objective:** Increase visibility and engagement on Brian's own LinkedIn posts by systematically engaging with his target audience's content first.

**Sales Navigator Search Filters:**
- Same audiences as Campaigns 1-3 but focus on profiles who **post actively**
- Use "Posted on LinkedIn in the last 30 days" filter in Sales Navigator

**Linked Helper Workflow:** Custom campaign

| Step | Action | Delay | Details |
|------|--------|-------|---------|
| 1 | **Visit profile** | - | Daily profile visits |
| 2 | **Like their post** | - | Automatic engagement |
| 3 | **Comment on post** | 1 day | Thoughtful comment (manually review these) |

**Note:** This campaign runs in parallel with the others. It warms up your audience so when they see your content, they're more likely to engage back. Linked Helper can auto-like, but **comments should be manually crafted** for authenticity.

## Content Strategy (To Support All Campaigns)

Post **3-4 times per week** using this rotation:

| Day | Content Type | Example |
|-----|-------------|---------|
| **Monday** | Educational tip | "HP vs PCP vs HP with a Balloon — which one actually suits your situation?" |
| **Tuesday** | Personal story / behind the scenes | "Had a call this week from a joiner in Stockport. He just wanted someone to explain the numbers in plain English..." |
| **Thursday** | Myth-busting / opinion piece | "A good finance deal is NOT just about the lowest monthly payment. Here's why..." |
| **Friday** | Engagement post / question | "Quick question: When you last financed a vehicle, did you feel like you fully understood what you were signing?" |

**Content Rules:**
- Always write as YOU, not as a brand - your personality IS the brand
- Mix professional knowledge with straight-talking Northern honesty
- Use real (anonymised) examples and numbers - specifics build trust
- End every post with a question or CTA to drive comments
- Reply to EVERY comment within 2 hours
- Use 3-5 relevant hashtags: #VehicleFinance #CarFinance #Manchester #SoleTrader #SelfEmployed

## Linked Helper Daily Limits (Stay Safe)

To avoid LinkedIn restrictions, set these **daily limits** in Linked Helper:

| Action | Daily Limit |
|--------|------------|
| Connection requests | 20-25/day (start at 10, increase gradually over 2 weeks) |
| Messages | 30-40/day |
| Profile visits | 60-80/day |
| Post likes | 40-50/day |
| Follow profiles | 20-30/day |
| Skill endorsements | 10-15/day |

**Important:** Start ALL limits at 50% of these numbers for the first 2 weeks, then gradually increase. LinkedIn flags sudden spikes in activity.

## Getting Started Checklist

1. **Optimise your LinkedIn profile first** - headline should be benefit-driven (e.g., "I Help People in the North West Save Money on Vehicle Finance | Personal Service, Multiple Lenders")
2. **Install Linked Helper 2** and connect it to your LinkedIn
3. **Start with Campaign 1** (Tradespeople) only - get comfortable with the tool
4. **Add Campaign 4** (Content Engagement) in week 2
5. **Launch Campaign 2** (Referral Network) in week 3
6. **Add Campaign 3** (Personal Car Buyers) in month 2
7. **Explore Campaign 5** (Groups) in month 2-3
8. **Post content consistently** from day 1 - this is non-negotiable

---

## Verification / Measuring Success

- **Week 1:** Check Linked Helper dashboard for connection request acceptance rates. Aim for >25%. If below 15%, revise connection messages.
- **Week 2:** Review message reply rates. If below 8%, shorten messages and make them more conversational.
- **Month 1:** Assess how many real conversations have turned into phone calls or enquiries. Minimum target: 5 genuine leads.
- **Monthly:** Review Sales Navigator "Social Selling Index" (SSI) score - aim to increase by 5-10 points per month.
- **Ongoing:** Track which campaign generates the best quality leads and double down on it.
